The image below is the EUR/USD 4H Japanese-Candlesticks chart, which is a price chart used by traders to track the exchange rate of the euro against the US dollar. It displays the historical price movements of the EUR/USD currency pair over a certain period of time, such as days, weeks, or months, and can be used to analyze market trends and identify potential trading opportunities. By examining the chart, traders can gain insight into the strength of the euro and the US dollar, as well as the overall sentiment of the forex market.
